### 🛠️ Things I've built & shipped

**🧠 [Feynman AI](https://github.com/elena-projects/feynman-ai)** → [try it](https://feynman.elenaprojects.cc)
Learn anything by *teaching it to an AI*. It plays your student, asks Socratic questions that expose your gaps, then scores your fluency & mastery.

**📚 [CogniCard](https://github.com/elena-projects/cognicard)** → [try it](https://cognicard.elenaprojects.cc)
Turn dense reading into a zoomable **mind map** — with an ADHD-friendly neural read-aloud focus mode, spaced-repetition review and a self-test quiz.
